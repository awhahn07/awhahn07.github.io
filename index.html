Intro

I am a technical leader, creative problem solver, and AI/ML engineer, with over 10 years of delivering mission success in the military and National Defense sectors. I am a transitioning Naval Officer that is looking to bring my vision and dedication to the private sector.

After graduating from the Unites States Naval Academy with a BS in Systems Engineering, I spent the next 5 years as a Surface Warfare Officer leading high-performance teams onboard USS SIMPSON (FFG 56) and at Coastal Riverine Squadron THREE (CRS 3) over three operational deployments. Here I honed my ability to lead by example of excellence through a strong command over technical detail and a broad understanding of strategic vision, mission, and goals. I bring this culture of excellence, dedication, and mission success to every project I undertake.

I left the warfighting community to pursue my passion for engineering. I earned a MS in Electrical Engineering from the Naval Postgraduate School, where I focused my studies and research in autonomous systems and AI/ML. I published my research in Machine Learning to the International Conference for Signal Processing and Communications Systems (ICPCS â€™19) and received the John M Wozencraft Award for Academic Excellence in Electrical and Computer Engineering for outstanding graduate research.

After graduation from NPS, I checked on board to the Position, Navigation, and Timing Lab at Naval Information Warfare Center Atlantic as the Lead Systems Engineer and Project Manager for Unmanned Surface Vessel Navigation sensors. At NIWC Atlantic, I continued my technical leadership and built a cross-functional engineering team to develop software and hardware for transition of the USV navigation suite to full autonomy.

In 2020, I founded AllCoast Digital, LLC, a machine learning research and engineering consulting firm. We are currently working with academia to advance state of the art signal processing and computer vision algorithms. AllCoast is dedicated to advancing state of the art and enabling autonomous systems with AI/ML.
